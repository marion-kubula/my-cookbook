how to cook kfc deep fried chicken

image

ingredients

  • 1kg of chicken
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • black and white pepper
  • grated ginger and garlic
  • rose mary
  • bread crumbs

    • step to follow

    1. mix ginger,ginger,flour,black and white pepper
    2. deep the chicken into the mix
    3. leave it to marinate for 30 minutes
    4. pass it into the egg
    5. pass through bread crumbs
    6. then deep fry
